📌 What is Connection Tracker?

Connection Tracker is a lightweight Chrome extension that lets you attach your own private notes to any LinkedIn profile. Instead of trying to remember where you met someone—or why you meant to reach out—simply write a quick note and keep everything in one place. Perfect for job seekers, recruiters, founders, and networkers who want context at their fingertips.

Add to Chrome — Free

⚙️ How It Works

  • Browse to any LinkedIn profile.
  • The extension instantly grabs the person’s name and profile URL for you.
  • Type your note — next steps, where you met, reminders, anything.
  • Hit Save. Your note lives safely in your browser’s local storage, ready in your extension.

🧠 Why Take Notes?

• Follow up with precision and warmth.
• Never blank on names or shared interests.
• Keep outreach organized without complicated CRMs.
• All data stays on your device — no accounts, no cloud.

🔐 Your Data, Your Control

Everything you write is stored locally in your browser’s local storage. Nothing is sent to external servers, and nobody can see your notes but you.

Keep in mind local storage is tied to your current browser and device. If you clear site data, use incognito mode, or switch devices, your notes won’t come along automatically.

🚀 Light‑weight & Distraction‑free

Connection Tracker adds under 50 KB to your browser and stays silent until you need it. No pop‑ups, no tracking, just the context you want—exactly when you want it.
